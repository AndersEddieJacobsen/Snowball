Energy saving
made easy.

Turning off the lights
for a brighter future.

GPS energy saver is an app that connects you smartphone with your smarthome. The GPS registers when you leave the house and will turn down the heating and turn off the lights. When you get closer to home, the heating starts up again. When you go to sleep, the GPS registers that you are in your bed-room, and turns down the heating until set time.
